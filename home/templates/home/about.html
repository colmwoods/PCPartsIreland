About PCPartsIreland

PCPartsIreland was created to provide a focused, user-friendly platform for purchasing computer hardware within Ireland.

The platform specialises exclusively in PC components including CPUs, GPUs, RAM, Storage, Motherboards, Power Supplies, Cases, Prebuilt PCs, and Accessories.

By concentrating purely on hardware, the store delivers a structured and simplified browsing experience compared to large international marketplaces.

Built using Django and PostgreSQL, the application combines secure Stripe payments, stock-aware ordering, and account management features to create a streamlined and trustworthy shopping experience.
