About PCPartsIreland

PCPartsIreland was created to provide a focused, user-friendly platform for purchasing computer hardware within Ireland.

PCPartsIreland is an Irish online store dedicated to supplying high-quality computer hardware for builders, gamers, students, and professionals. We specialise in essential PC components including CPUs, GPUs, RAM, Storage devices, Motherboards, Power Supplies, Cases, Prebuilt PCs, and Accessories — everything you need to build, upgrade, or customise your system.

Whether you're assembling your first PC, upgrading for better performance, or putting together a reliable office setup, our goal is to provide a focused and straightforward shopping experience. By concentrating exclusively on computer hardware, we make it easier to browse components without the clutter of unrelated products.

We believe PC building should be accessible, exciting, and stress-free. That’s why our store is structured around clear categories, transparent stock availability, and a smooth checkout process — helping you move from research to completed build with confidence.

From high-performance gaming GPUs to dependable storage solutions and efficient power supplies, PCPartsIreland aims to support every stage of your build. Whether you're a hobbyist, competitive gamer, student, or professional, we’re here to help you find the right components for your needs.

PCPartsIreland is committed to serving the Irish tech community by offering a dedicated platform built around performance, reliability, and simplicity.
